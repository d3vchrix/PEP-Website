Integrated Marketing
Communications

In June 2023, the Department of Tourism National Capital Region commissioned Positive Exposure to rebrand the Metro Manila Tourism program to be more relevant to its target market and align with the newly launched national branding campaign, Love the Philippines. This will include the development of a sustained social media program and strategic content plus rebranding of all internal and external communications, merchandising and collaterals to reflect the new branding handle.
It All Starts Here
Through a series of workshops and consultations and supplemented by research and data analysis, Positive Exposure developed a comprehensive marketing and communications plan driven by two compelling insights:

  • that Metro Manila is the premiere gateway and that your experience in the Philippines begins once you land in Metro Manila
  • that Metro Manila is an innovator and at times disruptor that develops products that go beyond the traditional
Through the collaborative effort of the Department of Tourism National Capital Region and Positive Exposure, the launch of the new brand handle was positively embraced by the sector and stakeholders.
