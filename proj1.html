Thank God It’s the Weekend Travel Show (TGIW) (2019)

A 30-minute weekly lifestyle and travel television program produced by Positive Exposure Productions, Inc., designed to showcase the Philippines’ diverse tourism destinations and rich culinary heritage. Hosted by renowned travel personality Ms. Maggie Wilson, the program features curated travel experiences, local culture, gastronomy, and destination stories that highlight the country’s unique offerings to both domestic and international audiences. The program was launched as a 10-episode season distributed across digital platforms and aired on CNN Philippines in 2019, where it was recognized for its premium production value and strong audience engagement. Through immersive storytelling, destination features, and personality-driven narratives, TGIW contributed to strengthening tourism awareness and positioning Philippine destinations within a modern lifestyle and travel context. As part of Positive Exposure’s integrated tourism communications initiatives, the program served as a strategic media platform supporting destination promotion, stakeholder visibility, and experiential tourism storytelling aligned with national tourism development objectives.
