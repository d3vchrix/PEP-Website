OVERVIEW
In 2024, Positive Exposure and it’s Joint Venture Partner, CEST Inc., were awarded the Tourism Development Planning requirements for Region 7 which included Cebu, Bohol, Negros Oriental and Siquijor. This eight month development plan. The Regional Tourism Development Plan was created in harmony with the National Tourism Development Plan.

The general approach consists of the creation of planning clusters, formulation of a tourism development framework, identification of tourism infrastructure planning and development and preparation of a Branding and Marketing plan for each province.

REGION 7 REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT PLAN
Through research, focus group discussions, workshops, consultations and actual site surveys, the joint venture team were able to develop the framework for the Tourism Development Plan which covered the Situational Analysis, Regional Tourism Development Framework and Marketing Strategies for the four identified provinces. Through the accumulated experience in the management of tourism development planning, Positive Exposure was subsequently awarded the Tourism Development Plan creation for Region 3 ( Central Luzon ) and the National Capital Region.
