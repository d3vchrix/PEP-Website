Tourism Development
Consulting

Positive Exposure Productions, Inc. is a tourism development and consulting organization with demonstrated capacity in planning, product innovation, infrastructure development, and program implementation aligned with national and international tourism frameworks. As a practicing member of the Global Sustainable Tourism Council (GSTC), the organization adheres to globally recognized standards on sustainable destination management and supports tourism initiatives that are consistent with the ASEAN Tourism Strategic Direction.

The company provides end-to-end technical assistance in tourism development, including destination assessment, tourism master planning, product and experience design, tourism mobility and infrastructure integration, digital tourism systems development, and stakeholder engagement. Its work is guided by the principles of balancing People, Planet, Prosperity, Peace, and Partnership, recognizing these as foundational pillars for building a resilient and thriving tourism sector at the national and local levels.

Positive Exposure maintains a strong pool of multidisciplinary experts and industry practitioners with experience across tourism policy, transport and mobility systems, destination marketing, investment structuring, and sustainable operations. The organization likewise sustains strategic partnerships with tourism institutions, private sector operators, and development specialists across various regions, enabling the exchange of relevant policies, best practices, and innovative solutions that support inclusive and competitive tourism growth.

Through these capabilities, Positive Exposure Productions, Inc. positions itself as a reliable partner for governments, tourism boards, local government units, and private stakeholders seeking to develop future-ready tourism ecosystems anchored on sustainability, accessibility, and shared economic value
