Technology Solutions

In its commitment to contribute to the transformation and revitalization of the Philippine tourism sector in the post-pandemic environment, Positive Exposure Productions, Inc. has strengthened its technology solutions capabilities to support the shift toward digitally enabled and future-ready tourism systems. Recognizing the opportunities presented by the so-called “new normal,” the organization has aligned its initiatives with the strategic focus areas of the World Tourism Organization (UNWTO), particularly in advancing Innovation, Education, Digital Transformation, and Investments as key drivers of tourism recovery, competitiveness, and sustainability. Guided by these principles, Positive Exposure has established strategic partnerships with several leading technology providers in the Philippines and across Asia to develop and deploy smart tourism destination system solutions. These initiatives include the implementation of user navigation and digital tourism experience platforms that enhance accessibility and seamless journey planning; command-and-response and monitoring systems that strengthen local government capabilities in tourism management, safety, and operational coordination; smart transport systems utilizing electric vehicles to promote environmentally responsible and low-emission mobility; and connectivity infrastructure such as outdoor mesh Wi-Fi networks that enable continuous digital access across tourism sites and corridors.

Through an integrated approach that combines technology sourcing, systems integration, stakeholder onboarding, and operational deployment, Positive Exposure supports tourism stakeholders in adapting to emerging digital trends while building innovation-driven ecosystems that foster entrepreneurship, economic growth, job creation, and sustainable destination development. By leveraging its network of technology partners and multidisciplinary expertise, the organization positions itself as a key enabler of smart tourism transformation in the Philippines.
