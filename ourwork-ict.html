Technology Solutions

Through years of experience in tourism product development, a deep understanding of the tourism value chain, forged partnerships with tourism stakeholders, local government units and strategic government agencies, Positive Exposure embarked on a major initiative to digitalize the tourism sector. Envisioned as a testament of public private partnership in the tourism sector, the aim was to create an environment that was collaborative, efficient and addresses the needs of the sector and the visitors. This internally funded digital initiative is positioned to be the first complete and inclusive travel platform focused on the Philippines.

The Localized Online Visitor Ecosystem ( LovePH ) is a fully integrated Philippine travel planning and booking platform integrating travel tools such as documentation, planning, booking to payment for travel to the Philippines. It aligns with the National Tourism goals of showcasing our heritage and culture with emphasis on the Philippine Experience.

Our vision is to make Philippine travel a satisfying journey by connecting every visitor to authentic experiences while growing local communities through a unified seamless digital platform. Our end goal is to unite the Philippine tourism ecosystem with both public and private stakeholders around one shared platform to market the Philippines as a world-class destination. Developing our backend platforms world-class technology companies Aqwire and Multysis ( a PLDT company ).

LovePh is supported by the Department of Tourism, the various tourism stakeholders and strategic partners from the private sector.
